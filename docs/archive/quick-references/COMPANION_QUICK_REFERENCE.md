## Ability Quick Reference

### Must-Have Abilities
- **Track** - Essential for hunters and criminals (+15 tracking)
- **Guard** - Alert to danger (prevents ambushes)
- **Attack** - Basic combat damage (+18 power)
- **Loyal Defense** - Protect player (requires 80 loyalty)
- **Scout** - Reveal area and threats
- **Pack Tactics** - Group bonuses

### Powerful Abilities
- **Feral Rage** - Berserker mode (+35 damage)
- **Thunder Strike** - Lightning attack (+45 damage)
- **Ghost Walk** - Phase through walls (perfect stealth)
- **Shape Shift** - Multiple forms (supernatural only)
- **Blood Drain** - Life steal (+30 damage + heal)

### Utility Abilities
- **Scavenge** - Find hidden items
- **Night Vision** - No darkness penalties
- **Keen Sight** - Improved discovery
- **Burrow Flush** - Hunt small game
- **Omen** - Reveal secrets

## Combat Role Bonuses

### Attacker
- Damage: +50% attack power
- Best for: High DPS builds
- Top species: Wolf Hybrid, Mountain Lion, Chupacabra

### Defender
- Damage Absorption: 50% of incoming
- Companion takes 80% of absorbed damage
- Best for: Survival builds
- Top species: German Shepherd, Bear Cub

### Support
- Damage Boost: +15% to player
- Defense Boost: +30% reduction
- No damage taken
- Best for: Balanced builds
- Top species: Australian Shepherd, Raven, Ghost Hound

### Scout
- Critical Bonus: Extra damage on high hands
- Reveals enemy info
- Best for: Exploration builds
- Top species: Bloodhound, Coydog, Ferret

## Taming Success Tips

1. **Max Spirit Stat** - Each point = +0.5% success
2. **Train Animal Handling** - Each level = +3% success
3. **Attempt Progression** - Each fail improves next attempt
4. **Difficulty Matters**:
   - Difficulty 1-3: 70-90% base chance
   - Difficulty 4-6: 40-60% base chance
   - Difficulty 7-9: 10-30% base chance
   - Difficulty 10: 0% base chance (requires bonuses)
5. **5 Attempts Max** - Plan your bonuses accordingly

## Training Times & Costs

- **Training Duration**: 24 hours (real-time)
- **Training Cost**: 10x ability power
  - Low power (10-15): 100-150g
  - Medium power (18-25): 180-250g
  - High power (30-40): 300-400g
  - Legendary power (45-50): 450-500g

## Healing Costs

- **Cost**: 2 gold per HP
- **Full Heal Examples**:
  - Small companion (40 HP): 80g
  - Medium companion (70 HP): 140g
  - Large companion (100 HP): 200g

## Pro Tips

1. **Start with Australian Shepherd or Ferret** - Best value early game
2. **Feed before combat** - Full companions perform better
3. **Train abilities ASAP** - 24 hour wait is long
4. **One companion active** - Switch based on activity
5. **Track bond carefully** - Neglect = companion leaves
6. **Use correct role** - Attackers for DPS, Defenders for survival
7. **Supernatural = endgame** - Don't rush, level requirements are high
8. **Taming is free** - No gold cost, just energy
9. **Max kennel = 3** - Choose wisely
10. **Devoted companions never leave** - Reach 80+ bond ASAP

## Emergency Care Checklist

### Critical (Red Alert)
- [ ] Hunger < 20: Feed immediately (costs daily food)
- [ ] Health < 30%: Heal immediately (costs 2g per HP)
- [ ] Not fed for 5+ days: Risk of leaving

### High Priority (Orange)
- [ ] Hunger < 40: Feed soon
- [ ] Health < 50%: Heal when possible
- [ ] Happiness < 30: Activate or play with

### Medium Priority (Yellow)
- [ ] Happiness < 50: Give attention
- [ ] Training complete: Collect new ability

### Low Priority (Green)
- [ ] Bond < 60: Build trust through activities
- [ ] No active companion: Set one active
