## Recommended Progression Path

### Early Game (Level 1-5)

1. **Start training Blacksmithing** (primary craft skill)
   - First skill to train for metal tools/weapons
   - 2h 12m passive training time per level

2. **Do Light Day Labor** at The Labor Exchange while training
   - 12 energy, 8-15 gold, 15 XP, 25m cooldown
   - Earns gold while waiting for skill training

3. **Explore Red Gulch** when buildings are open
   - Visit Iron Jake's Forge when available
   - Check Mei Ling's Laundry for Chinese Quarter access

4. **Travel to Goldfinger's Mine** to practice Mining skill once available

### Mid Game (Level 5-15)

1. **Diversify craft skills**:
   - Mining + Prospecting (resource gathering)
   - Herbalism + Alchemy (potions)
   - Carpentry (furniture/structures)

2. **Visit Wong Li** for mentor abilities:
   - Master Craftsmanship
   - Ancient Techniques
   - Rare Recipes

3. **Establish income streams**:
   - Craft items to sell at marketplace
   - Process raw materials for other players

### Late Game (Level 15+)

1. **Max key craft skills** to 50
2. **Join or create a gang** for crafting bonuses
3. **Own property** with crafting stations
4. **Legendary crafting** - rare recipes and materials

## Energy Management

- **Base Energy**: 150/150
- **Regen Rate**: +10.0/min
- **Travel Costs**: 3-15 energy depending on distance
- **Job Costs**: 10-50 energy per action

### Efficient Energy Use
- Train skills passively (no energy cost)
- Stack jobs with short cooldowns
- Minimize travel when possible
- Use buildings in current location first
