# Boss Encounters Guide

Master the art of facing down the most dangerous threats in the frontier.

---

## Overview

Boss encounters are multi-phase combat challenges against powerful named enemies. Unlike regular combat, bosses have unique abilities, multiple health phases, and require strategic thinking to defeat.

---

## Boss Types

### Story Bosses
Encountered during quest chains, these bosses mark major story milestones.
- Single-player encounters
- Scaled to your level
- Guaranteed drops on defeat
- Cannot be re-fought once completed

### Territory Bosses
Guard strategic locations and resources.
- Respawn on a timer after defeat
- Difficulty based on territory tier
- Drop territory-specific loot
- May require group effort

### World Bosses
Massive public encounters for many players.
- See [World Bosses](#world-bosses) section below
- Server-wide events
- Contribution-based rewards
- Weekly/biweekly spawns

### Legendary Hunts
Special single-player boss hunts.
- See [Legendary Hunts](#legendary-hunts) section below
- Discovery-based progression
- Unique permanent rewards
- Trophy collection system

## Multi-Phase Combat

### Phase System
Most bosses have 3-5 distinct phases:

1. **Phase 1** - Boss at full power, standard attacks
2. **Phase 2** - New abilities unlock, increased aggression
3. **Phase 3** - Desperation moves, summons minions
4. **Phase 4** - Enraged state, faster attacks
5. **Phase 5** (if applicable) - Final stand, all abilities available

### Phase Transitions
- Triggered when boss reaches HP thresholds (75%, 50%, 25%, 10%)
- Brief invulnerability during transition
- Boss may heal slightly or summon reinforcements
- New attack patterns begin

### Reading Phase Changes
Watch for these indicators:
- Boss health bar color changes
- Visual effects (aura, glow, stance change)
- Boss dialogue or sound cues
- UI notification of phase change

## Boss Abilities

### Ability Categories

| Type | Description | Counter Strategy |
|------|-------------|------------------|
| **DAMAGE** | Single-target attacks | Use defensive cards, armor |
| **AOE** | Area attacks hitting all | Spread out, use shields |
| **DOT** | Damage over time effects | Medicine skill, cleanse items |
| **SUMMON** | Spawns minion enemies | Kill adds quickly |
| **HEAL** | Boss recovers HP | Burst damage, interrupt |
| **BUFF** | Boss strengthens self | Dispel or wait out |
| **DEBUFF** | Weakens your character | Resistance items, cleanse |
| **STUN** | Prevents actions | Stun resistance gear |
| **ULTIMATE** | Massive signature attack | Survive mechanic, dodge |
| **ENVIRONMENTAL** | Uses terrain hazards | Position carefully |

### Status Effects
Bosses can inflict 16+ status effects:

**Damage Effects:**
- Bleed - Damage per turn
- Burn - Fire damage over time
- Poison - Escalating damage

**Control Effects:**
- Stun - Skip turn
- Slow - Reduced actions
- Blind - Reduced accuracy

**Debuff Effects:**
- Weakness - Reduced damage
- Vulnerability - Take more damage
- Exhaustion - Reduced energy

**Special Effects:**
- Fear - Cannot use certain abilities
- Curse - Reduced healing received
- Mark - Boss prioritizes you

### Status Effect Stacking
- Multiple effects can stack
- Same effect refreshes duration
- Some effects intensify when stacked
- Maximum 5 stacks per effect type

## Combat Strategy

### Preparation
Before engaging a boss:

1. **Check Level** - Ensure you meet minimum level
2. **Repair Gear** - Full durability on equipment
3. **Stock Consumables** - Healing items, buffs, cleansers
4. **Review Abilities** - Know what the boss does
5. **Save Energy** - Boss fights cost extra energy

### During Combat

**Phase 1 Strategy:**
- Learn attack patterns
- Save powerful abilities
- Build up resources/cards
- Note ability cooldowns

**Phase 2-3 Strategy:**
- Use medium-power abilities
- Watch for new mechanics
- Manage your health actively
- Handle adds immediately

**Phase 4+ Strategy:**
- Use ultimate abilities
- Pop all consumables if needed
- Focus on survival
- Push for kill before enrage timer

### Key Combat Tips
- **Card Management** - Hold key cards for critical moments
- **Ability Timing** - Reroll, Peek, Quick Draw strategically
- **Health Threshold** - Keep health above 25% for safety
- **Burst Windows** - Identify when boss is vulnerable

## World Bosses

### Current World Bosses

| Boss | HP | Max Players | Spawn Frequency | Min Contribution |
|------|-----|-------------|-----------------|------------------|
| **The Maw** | 50,000 | 20 | Weekly | 3% |
| **The Collector** | 75,000 | 50 | Weekly | 5% |
| **The Mirror** | 100,000 | 75 | Biweekly | 7% |
| **The Herald** | 200,000 | 100 | Biweekly | 10% |

### How World Bosses Work
1. Boss spawns at announced time (server notification)
2. Players travel to boss location
3. Combat is real-time, shared health pool
4. Your contribution is tracked as percentage of total damage
5. When boss dies, all contributors get rewards

### Contribution System
- Minimum contribution required for rewards
- Higher contribution = better reward tier
- Healing and support actions also count
- Tank/aggro role provides contribution bonus

### World Boss Rewards
- Scaling with contribution percentage
- Unique cosmetic items
- Rare crafting materials
- Significant gold and XP
- Achievement progress

## Legendary Hunts

### Discovery System
Legendary creatures must be discovered before hunting:

| Stage | Name | How to Progress |
|-------|------|-----------------|
| 1 | UNKNOWN | Creature not yet discovered |
| 2 | RUMORED | Hear rumors from NPCs |
| 3 | TRACKED | Find tracks/evidence in the wild |
| 4 | LOCATED | Discover creature's lair |

### Hunting Process
1. Gather rumors from NPCs in relevant regions
2. Search for tracks using Tracking skill
3. Follow clues to locate the creature
4. Challenge the legendary beast
5. Defeat it in single combat

### Legendary Hunt Rewards
- **Permanent Bonuses** - Stat increases that persist
- **Trophies** - Display in your property
- **Unique Items** - Legendary-quality gear
- **Achievements** - Bragging rights

## Boss-Specific Strategies

### General Tips by Boss Type

**Humanoid Bosses (Bandits, Outlaws):**
- Often use pistols - range matters
- May call reinforcements
- Vulnerable to flanking
- Use Deception skill for advantage

**Beast Bosses (Animals, Monsters):**
- High physical damage
- Often have charge attacks
- Tracking skill reveals weaknesses
- Animal Handling may reduce aggro

**Supernatural Bosses (Spirits, Cursed):**
- Immune to some physical damage
- Use elemental attacks
- Ritual Knowledge skill helps
- Spirit skills often effective

**Mechanical Bosses (Trains, Machines):**
- High armor values
- Vulnerable to sabotage
- Engineering skill reveals weak points
- Explosives highly effective

## Troubleshooting

### "I keep dying in Phase 2"
- Your gear may need upgrading
- Stock more healing consumables
- Learn the new Phase 2 abilities
- Consider leveling a few more times

### "I can't find the boss"
- Check quest log for location hints
- Use Tracking skill in the area
- Talk to NPCs for rumors
- Some bosses only spawn at certain times

### "The boss healed back to full"
- Boss likely has a heal mechanic
- Look for interruptible cast times
- May need to kill adds first
- Check for environmental healing sources

### "My damage isn't doing anything"
- Boss may have damage immunity phases
- Check for required mechanics
- Ensure you're meeting level requirements
- Some bosses require specific damage types
