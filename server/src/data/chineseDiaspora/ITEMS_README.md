# Chinese Diaspora Items & Intelligence System

Complete catalog of unique items and intelligence rewards available through the Chinese Diaspora network.

## Overview

The Chinese Diaspora network offers items and information that cannot be obtained anywhere else in the game. All items reflect authentic Chinese culture and history, providing mechanically unique benefits.

## Design Principles

1. **Cultural Authenticity**: Every item has historical/cultural context
2. **Mechanical Uniqueness**: Items provide benefits unavailable from Western sources
3. **Trust-Gated Access**: Better items require higher trust levels
4. **Intelligence as Currency**: Information is as valuable as gold
5. **Legendary Scarcity**: Some items are truly unique (only one exists)

## Trust Level Requirements

| Trust Level | English Name | Chinese Name | Items Available |
|------------|--------------|--------------|-----------------|
| 1 | Outsider | 外人 (Wài Rén) | None |
| 2 | Friend | 朋友 (Péng Yǒu) | Common items |
| 3 | Sibling | 兄弟/姐妹 (Xiōng Dì/Jiě Mèi) | Rare items |
| 4 | Family | 家人 (Jiā Rén) | Legendary items |
| 5 | Dragon | 龙 (Lóng) | Unique items |

## Price Modifiers by Trust Level

- **Trust 2 (Friend)**: 100% base price
- **Trust 3 (Sibling)**: 85% (15% discount)
- **Trust 4 (Family)**: 70% (30% discount)
- **Trust 5 (Dragon)**: 50% (50% discount)

## DESIGN NOTES

### Cultural Authenticity
Every item includes:
- English name
- Chinese name (Pinyin)
- Chinese characters
- Historical/cultural context note
- Authentic properties (e.g., silk armor blade resistance is historically accurate)

### Mechanical Uniqueness
Chinese items provide benefits unavailable elsewhere:
- Silk armor: no mobility penalty
- Traditional medicine: more effective than Western
- Acupuncture: can heal others
- Network intelligence: information Western characters cannot access
- Martial arts weapons: require skill training

### Trust Progression
Items become more powerful and unique as trust increases:
- **Trust 2**: Useful but common items
- **Trust 3**: Specialized items with unique mechanics
- **Trust 4**: Legendary items with powerful effects
- **Trust 5**: Unique items that only one player can possess at a time

### Intelligence as Reward
Information is as valuable as physical items:
- Low-level intel: tactical advantages (patrols, prices)
- Mid-level intel: strategic advantages (networks, secrets)
- High-level intel: game-changing knowledge (complete maps, blackmail, supernatural)

---

## QUEST INTEGRATION

Many unique items require quest chains:
1. **Celestial Dragon Sword**: 3-quest chain testing worthiness
2. **Emperor's Silk**: 3-quest chain about Imperial legacy
3. **Elixir of Immortality**: 3-quest chain proving worthy of second chance
4. **Dragon's Seal**: 3-quest chain becoming network champion

All quest-only items cannot be purchased with gold alone.

---

## BALANCE CONSIDERATIONS

1. **Price Scaling**: Items become cheaper as trust increases (50% off at Dragon level)
2. **Power Scaling**: Early items are useful but not overpowered
3. **Unique Limits**: Some items only one player can own at a time
4. **Skill Requirements**: Powerful weapons require martial arts training
5. **Intelligence Expiry**: Some intel expires, requiring repurchase
