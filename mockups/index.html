ðŸ’¡ Design Recommendations

Classic Western is perfect if you want to emphasize the historical setting and create a warm, inviting atmosphere. It appeals to players who value authenticity and thematic consistency.

Modern Minimal excels at accessibility and works beautifully across all devices. Choose this if your priority is broad appeal, mobile optimization, and clean UX.

Gritty Cinematic creates the most memorable, dramatic experience. It's ideal for players who want intensity and atmosphere, though it may be too dark for some users.

Hybrid Approach: You can also mix elements! For example, use Modern Minimal as the base UI structure (for accessibility), but incorporate Classic Western textures and colors for thematic consistency.
